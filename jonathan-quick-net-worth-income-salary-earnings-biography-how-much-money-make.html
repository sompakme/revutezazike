Originally born on January 21, 1986, Jonathan Douglas Quick is an American professional ice hockey goalie who plays for the Los Angeles Kings of the National Hockey League (NHL). Quick was chosen by the Los Angeles Kings with the 72nd overall selection in the 2005 NHL Entry Draft. Quick competed for the United States and won a silver medal in the 2010 Winter Olympics in Sochi. On June 11, 2012, he won his first Stanley Cup title with the Los Angeles Kings, and he won his second with the same team the following year. As the most valuable player in the 2012 Stanley Cup playoffs, he was given the Conn Smythe Trophy.
