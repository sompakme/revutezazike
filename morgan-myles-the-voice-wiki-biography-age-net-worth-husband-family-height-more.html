Morgan Myles (born July 28, 1987, age: 36 years, as of 2023) is a singer and songwriter from America. Morgan rose to fame and got public attention when her name was announced as a contestant on the NBC reality singing show “The Voice 2022“.

She is titled as the powerhouse vocalist who has released many singles songs earlier. She was already famous among country music lovers but even though she participated in “The Voice” to prove herself.

Who is Morgan Myles?

Morgan Myles added her name to the top 5 finalists of “The Voice 2022′. She competed in the reality singing song under the mentorship of her coach Camila Cabello. She was admired for singing the Finale song “Total Eclipse of the Heart” which aired on 12 December 2022.

Appearing on the shows as a soul singer Morgan has impressed the audience as well as the other popular singer in the industry. In 2020 Morgan became a winner of the Nashville Industry Music Awards 2020 when she released her debut album “Therapy”.

Biography (Childhood & Qualification)

Popular singer Morgan Myles grew up listening to Bonnie Raitt, Susan Tedeschi, Janis Joplin, and many others. She was born in Pennsylvania on 28 July 1987. As a kid, she got influenced by many female singers and songwriters and began working to become like them. She later developed her skills as a natural singer and a songwriter.

Some of her songs add notes that motivate, give strength, and empower. She began writing songs at a very young age from a deeper life perspective.

Morgan Myles completed her schooling in 2005 at Loyalsock Township High School. She then went to grab some experience in music and Studied performance and Voice at Berklee College of Music. She then went on to have her Bachelor’s degree in business Administration, Music Business from Belmont University.

In 2009 Morgan began working as a caretaker of Women named Amy Gowan who passed away due to ALS. She use to take care of her daily needs for many years. Amy passed away a couple of years ago.
