Ciara Bravo and Tom Holland Starring in a New Movie 'Cherry'

Get ready, guys, because two of Hollywood’s biggest stars are teaming up for a brand new movie together. Yep, we’re talking about Spider-Man star Tom Holland and Big Time Rush actress Ciara Bravo! Wow, talk about an epic duo.

According to Variety, the movie is called Cherry and it’s based off a novel written by Nico Walker. It’s about a war veteran who returns home from the army and unfortunately suffers from PTSD. The flick will be directed by Anthony and Joe Russo, and will also star Bill Skarsgard, Jack Reynor, Forrest Goodluck, Jeff Wahlberg, Michael Gandolfini and Kyle Harvey.

As fans know, Ciara, 22, is most famous for her role as Kendall’s younger sister, Katie, in Big Time Rush. But she’s definitely come a long way since then! She went on to star in Red Band Society, Second Chance, Neighbors 2: Sorority Rising, To the Bone, The Long Dumb Road, Wayne and more.

As for Tom, he’s most famous for his role as Spider-Man in Spider-Man: Homecoming, Spider-Man: Far From Home and three Avenger movies. The 23-year-old also has a ton of other new films that he’s currently working on, including Disney/Pixar’s Onward, The Voyage of Doctor Dolittle, Spies in Disguise, The Devil All the Time, Chaos Walking and Uncharted. It was also announced last month that he’d be returning as the superhero we all know and love in another Spider-Man flick! That news was especially exciting because it had been reported in August that his character was being cut from Marvel’s franchise, after the company got into a feud with Sony.

We’re so glad that they were able to work things out in the end, and we can’t wait to see all of the movies he’s working on — especially Cherry. There’s unfortunately no release date yet, but production is currently underway.
