See Emily Ratajkowski Step Out in a Sheer Slipdress and Cowboy Boots

Emily Ratajkowski’s latest off-duty look is a breezy take on several celeb-loved trends.

The model and My Body author stepped out in New York City yesterday wearing a casual spin on both the lingerie-as-clothing and visible underwear trends. The ensemble consisted of a sheer, asymmetrical-hem slipdress from Burberry, trimmed with black lace and printed with the brand’s iconic black, tan, and red plaid pattern. In a chic and sexy twist, the model’s black bra and underwear were visible through the light fabric.

She completed the look with a pair of black cowboy boots, a caramel leather shoulder bag from the Row, oval-shaped sunglasses, and an array of gold jewelry, including large hoops and a nameplate necklace.

Gotham//Getty Images

The High Low With EmRata host has stepped out in several enviable street-style looks this spring. Earlier this week, she was spotted in a chic, body-sculpting vest shirt, which featured a peplum silhouette, hook-and-eye buttons down the center, and a plunging neckline. She coordinated the piece with straight-leg khaki trousers in a khaki shade, a pair of white Superga sneakers, wiry gold-frame eyeglasses, and a black cord choker necklace with an amber pendant.

Last month, the 2022 Harper’s Bazaar cover star took a style cue from Sex and the City, wearing a sultry spin on Carrie Bradshaw’s iconic newspaper dress, courtesy of Miaou. She styled the piece—which included a ruffled asymmetrical hem, a halter neck, and a backless silhouette—with pointy knee-high leather Dior boots in a rich caramel tone. Her accessories included a black leather shoulder bag from Miu Miu, squared black sunglasses from DMY by DMY, and gold hoop earrings by Jordan Road.
