Early Life & Education

Born on December 17, 1997, Ivorian Doll‘s origins trace back to Ivory Coast – her parents’ home country. But interestingly enough, she was actually born in Flensburg, Germany. Her mother, Honoria Mary Victoria Riddell, and her father, Erwin Stein, are a big part of her life – and her dad is a pastor, no less!

At the tender age of three, Ivorian Doll‘s family packed up and moved to East London, England, where she spent most of her childhood. Little did she know that her unique blend of talent, determination, and creativity would lead her to become the hip-hop icon that we all know and love today!

During her early years, Ivorian Doll attended Haggerston School and laid the groundwork for her future as an artist.

Career

Before taking the UK music scene by storm, Ivorian Doll made a name for herself as a YouTuber. But it wasn’t long before she realized that her true calling was music, and she quickly transitioned to the world of hip-hop.

Her star began to rise with some help from her partnership with Abigail Asante. And in 2019, she made her solo debut with Queen of Drill, a track that showcased her incredible talent and paved the way for her ascent to the top of the game.

Ivorian Doll‘s love for music was fueled by some of the greatest names in the industry, including Nicki Minaj, Foxy Brown, and Lil’ Kim. Their influence is evident in her music, which is a powerful fusion of classic hip-hop and contemporary beats.

But it was Ivorian Doll‘s hit track Rumours that truly put her on the map. The song was an instant sensation, garnering over a million YouTube views within weeks. And she didn’t stop there – she continued to release hit after hit, including Body Rag and Clout, all while collaborating with some of the biggest names in the industry.

In 2021, Ivorian Doll took the stage at the Wireless Festival and the Reading Festival, captivating audiences with her unique sound and undeniable charisma. Her performances were nothing short of electric, and she proved once again that she is one of the most exciting artists in the game today.
