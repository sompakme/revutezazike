- Floyd Mayweather released a video on his social media account training his son

Floyd Mayweather has begun a journey with his son Koraun in boxing as he revealed the latest video clip of them training together on Instagram.

The 21-year-old was wearing boxing gloves while Mayweather was teaching him foot-work in the video clip.

Mayweather was on his toes as he threw as left-handed strikes as Koraun tested his punch power on the pads.
