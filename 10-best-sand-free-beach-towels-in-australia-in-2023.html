WHAT ARE SAND-FREE TOWELS?

Sand-free towels are made from materials like velour or microfibre which have fine, loop-free fibres that prevent sand from being collected between them – it simply slides right off so you don’t have to stress about whipping out the car vacuum.

HOW DO SAND-RESISTANT TOWELS WORK?

It’s all about the material – sand-repellent towels are made from materials with short fibres and no loops, so there’s no place for sand to get itself stuck in – it just slips right off.

ARE SAND FREE TOWELS WORTH IT?

For the mess they prevent, they’re absolutely worth it, according to plenty of enthusiastic buyers.

If you prefer a fluffy or heavier weight towel to dry off with after swimming, some sand free towels will feel different to a traditional style beach towel.
