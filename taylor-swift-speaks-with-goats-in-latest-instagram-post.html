Taylor Swift Speaks With Goats in Latest Instagram Post

What's the rudest creature in the animal kingdom? If you ask Taylor Swift, the answer may very well be goats. The singer posted a hilarious video to her Instagram yesterday, in which she tried rather unsuccessfully to reason with a group of goats.

In the clip, Swift, dressed in printed leggings and a Wildfox sweatshirt, attempts to speak with a flock of goats, although she is quickly cut off by one of the animals, who interrupts her with a "baa." But Swift took the interaction as a learning opportunity: She captioned the video, "Important lessons from today: 1) Sheep are impossible to reason with. 2) Sheep are rude."
