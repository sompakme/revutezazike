How Did Small Change of $5000 Become N9m? Paul Okoye of Psquare Groans, Flaunts Money

  • Paul Okoye of Psquare has reacted to the huge gap between the dollar and naira in the country
  • In a recent video, the music star lamented how a 'small change' of $5000 was now N9 million
  • As expected, Paul Okoye's video has triggered reactions from Nigerians as many shared their takes on the situation of the economy

Popular singer and member of Psquare music group Paul Okoye, aka Rude Boy, has joined other Nigerian netizens to cry out over the growing exchange rate gap between the dollar and the naira.

Over the past few days, the naira and dollar conversion rate has been a heated topic among Nigerians on and off social media amid the current economic situation.

He, however, groaned at how the dollars, when converted to Nigerian currency, was now N9 million.

"So you are telling me this small change of 5000 dollars is now nine million naira, I don't know whether make I dey happy or make I dey sad," the singer said in the video.

Sharing the video, Paul Okoye wrote in a caption:

"How small change became a mighty change."
