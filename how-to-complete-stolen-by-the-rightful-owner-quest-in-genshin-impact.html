How to complete Stolen by the Rightful Owner quest in Genshin Impact

In Stolen by the Rightful Owner quest, Travelers will have to help Taliesin find the ring that has been stolen from him. Here's how you can complete this quest.

First Published April 1, 2022, 15:30

Genshin Impact 2.6 update has arrived with a ton of new world quests, and one of them is the Stolen By The Rightful Owner. In this quest, you will have to help Taliesin, a bard, find the ring he stole and is now stolen from him. 

This quest takes place in The Chasm and will get unlocked after you complete the Surreptitious Seven-Star Seal Sundering quest in the game.

Travelers will be rewarded with 200 Adventure Experience, 30 Primogems, 20k Mora, and two Hero's Wit upon completing Stolen By The Rightful Owner. Here's a step by step guide on how you can complete this quest.

Genshin Impact: Stolen By The Rightful Owner

You will have to go to Jinwu's camp and talk to Taliesin in order to start the Stolen By The Rightful Owner, who will ask you to find the ring that got stolen from him. 

Then, you will have to go to the marked location in Ghaze Peak in Liyue by teleporting to the nearest teleport waypoint marked on the map below. 

There you will find the Weasel Thief Training Manual near a camp, and once you interact with it, you will have to stand on the rock just behind you and whistle. Don't forget to set the time to 00:30 by going into the Paimon Menu.

After you whistle, the Golden Weasel Thief will appear that you will have to kill quickly to obtain the Brass Ring. 

Then, you will have to go back to Taliesin and give him the ring to complete the quest.
