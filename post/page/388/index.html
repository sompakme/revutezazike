What do Aussies put on the barbie?

published on July 14, 2024
"Barbie" is Australian slang for barbecue and the phrase "slip a shrimp on the barbie" often evokes images of a fun social gathering under the sun. Australians, however, invariably use the word prawn rather than shrimp. Do Australians say put another shrimp on the barbie? This saying means to cook a shrimp (a prawn) on a barbecue.
