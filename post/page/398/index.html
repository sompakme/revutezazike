What are some of the things you might find in a behaviour support plan?

published on July 12, 2024
BSPs may include: known triggers of the behaviour (noise, touch, language used)situations that make the behaviour more likely or cause the behaviour to occur (hunger, tiredness, pain)strategies to reduce or remove triggers.strategies to address situations that may trigger the behaviour. What should a behavior support plan include? A "Behavior Support Plan" (BSP) is a plan that assists a member in building positive behaviors to replace or reduce a challenging/dangerous behavior.
