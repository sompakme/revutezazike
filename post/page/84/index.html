Spotify Adds Showtime To Their $4.99 Student Subscription Package

published on September 15, 2024
Spotify continues to bless students with the ultimate entertainment deal. Last year, the streaming service announced that eligible students would be able to get Hulu as a part of the $5 student package. According to Variety, Spotify is now adding Showtime to the deal as well. For just $4.99, students can get Spotify premium, Hulu's limited commercial plan, and Showtime. “Spotify Premium has just changed the content game for students. By joining forces with Showtime and continuing our relationship with Hulu, Spotify Premium is now able to give students a complete world of music and video content, shows, and film,” stated Spotify chief premium officer Alex Norström.
