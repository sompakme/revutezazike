The Gap x LoveShackFancy Collaboration 2023: Everything You Need to Buy

ICYMI, there's a collaboration everyone can't stop talking about: the Gap x LoveShackFancy collection. At first, LoveShackFancy's signature floral, lacy silhouettes joining forces with Gap's laid-back Americana vibe seemed unconventional, but now that we've seen the full collection, it all makes sense. The 76-piece capsule includes everything you could think of, from tiered maxidresses to floral printed hoodies, matching tote bags, corset tops, and baseball caps.

Gap x LoveShackFancy Kids Rosette Hair Tie

Gap × LoveShackFancy Floral Shrunken T-Shirt

An honorable mention goes to the rosette phenomenon, which makes an appearance with dainty decals on denim jackets, and 3D scrunchies for little ones. Another huge trend part of the collection is the ballet flats. But what's really making everyone excited for this drop is its accessibility and size inclusivity. A typical LoveShackFancy dress can set you back as far as $700; a nubby-knit sweater about $400, with sizes going up to 14 or XXL for some styles. The Gap collection ranges from $13 to $248—a tremendous difference. And as for sizing, most Gap x LSF pieces are available from XXS through 2XL, with hoodies going up to 3XL. 

LoveShackFancy's ultra-feminine aesthetic shines through thanks to hues of pink, baby blue, and canary yellow found throughout every single piece. And Gap's iconic denim is seen across the collection, even mixed in with some florals (like this adorable kids' jacket). And to no one's surprise, all the pretty vibes are selling out pretty quickly. To shop the Gap LoveShackFancy collab while you can, start with our edit of must-haves below.
