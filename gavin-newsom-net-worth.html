Gavin Newsom Net Worth

Gavin Newsom is an American politician and Democrat who was born on October 10, 1967 in San Francisco, CA. He served as the 42nd Mayor of San Francisco from 2004 to 2011, and then became the Lieutenant Governor of California in 2011. During his time as Mayor, he implemented the popular Care Not Cash program, which helps move homeless people into city-assisted care.
