How do you reset the tire pressure light on a Hyundai Santa Fe?

published on August 07, 2024
Answer Turn the ignition key to the “On” position without starting the vehicle. Press and hold the TPMS reset button for three seconds or until the light blinks three times, and then release the button. Start the vehicle and let it run for 20 minutes to allow the sensor to be reset and calibrated.
