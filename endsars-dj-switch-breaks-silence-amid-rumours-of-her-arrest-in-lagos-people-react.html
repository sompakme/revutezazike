EndSARS DJ Switch Breaks Silence Amid Rumours of Her Arrest in Lagos, People React

  • DJ Switch has reacted to reports of her arrest by the Nigerian police in Lagos state
  • The disc jockey was reported to have been arrested and charged to court for assaulting a police officer
  • Following the viral claims, the DJ threatened to take legal action against those behind the report

Popular Disk Jockey Obianuju Catherine Udeh, aka DJ Switch, has reacted to the trending report of her arrest by the police in Lagos.

The report, which recently emerged online, claimed the DJ who became popular during the ENDSARS campaign in 2020 had been taken to the magistrate court in Eti Osa.

Linda Ikeji's Blog claimed Switch was arraigned for obstructing police officers while on duty and assaulting them.

Reactions trailed DJ Switch's rumoured arrest

The rumours left many of the disc jockey's fans worried, as they took to social media to express concerns.

DJ Switch says she was never in Canada

Meanwhile, Legit.ng reported that DJ Switch took to social media to speak about her rumoured asylum in Canada following her role in the #EndSARS movement in October of 2020.
