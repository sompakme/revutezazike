Blue Ivy Carter's net worth revealed as she attends Wearable Art Gala auction

Blue Ivy Carter recently bid over $80k at the recent Wearable Art Gala auction and it does not come as a surprise because her net worth is mind-blowing.

The 2022 Wearable Art Gala was a night to remember as there were several moments that caught the attention of, everyone.

Right from Beyonce confirming her world tour to Blue Ivy bidding over $80k, it was definitely a night that was hard to forget.

Blue Ivy Carter bids during the recent auction

Blue Ivy has actively taken part in auctions and things were no different in 2022 when she attended the Wearable Art Gala event with her parents.

In a video that went viral on social media, Keke Palmer, Tina Knowles, and Quinta Brunson were on stage during the bidding war. It is unclear what the item was.

However, they could be seen urging people to take part in the auction. As the video progresses, the ladies were convinced that $80k was the final bid. However, Blue Ivy shocked everyone when she decided to raise the price.

What is Blue Ivy’s net worth?

As per The Sun, Blue Ivy has an estimated net worth of $500 million and is among one of the eight richest kids in America.

Ever since Blue Ivy was born she had made headlines. In fact, she was also noted to be the most famous baby in the world” by Time Magazine.

She has appeared in a couple of music videos of Beyonce and even accompanied her during the Super Bowl halftime performance.

At the same time, she has also won a Grammy and a BET award.

This is not the first time she has taken part in the auction

Blue Ivy is no stranger to auctions as back in 2018 she bid $10,000 and won.

As per Vanity Fair, Blue Ivy was attending the Wearable Art Gala in Los Angeles in 2018 along with her parents.

During the event, she made a number of bids. While most of them she was outbid by others she eventually managed to win a 36” x 36” piece of art created by artist Samuel Levi Jones.
