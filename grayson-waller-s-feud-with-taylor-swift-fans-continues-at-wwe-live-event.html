Grayson Wallers Feud with Taylor Swift Fans Continues at WWE Live Event

Grayson Waller, the Australian WWE Superstar known for his penchant for stirring the pot, is back at it again, and this time, he’s not holding back in his feud with pop sensation Taylor Swift.

Waller made waves recently when he rated Taylor Swift as ‘6’ and suggested that NFL star Travis Kelce could do better. This led to an outpouring of anger from Swifties, the devoted fanbase of the global superstar. Waller, ever the provocateur, embraced the controversy.

During a WWE live event in Laredo, Waller took his feud with Taylor Swift to the next level. He made quite the entrance, donning a Taylor Swift shirt as he made his way to the ring. Alongside his partner Austin Theory, Waller took aim at Swift’s special handshake with Brittany Mahomes, which the singer had performed after a touchdown during the Kansas City Chiefs’ victory over the Los Angeles Chargers on October 22.

But Waller didn’t stop there; he also created fake apology video, further taunting Taylor Swift’s fans. With each move, Waller seems intent on keeping the feud alive and thriving.

As the rivalry between Grayson Waller and Taylor Swift continues to heat up, wrestling fans are left wondering if this could lead to a WWE program involving the pop superstar in the future. Regardless of the outcome, Waller’s dedication to his role as WWE’s ultimate provocateur is undeniable.
