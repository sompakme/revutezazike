Keira Knightley's fashionable 24 hours in six different looks

Keira Knightley has had a very fashion-focused 24 hours, wearing not one but six stylish looks to complete a series of interviews and television appearances in New York.

The actress – who is Harper's Bazaar's cover star this month – kicked things off in a three-piece Celine trouser suit, which she teamed with a ribbon-tie shirt and black boots. Next, she opted for more tailoring, changing into a cream Gucci skirt suit, paired with a blue button-up shirt, a thin black belt and matching heels.

Getty Images

Raymond Hall//Getty Images

Knightley then switched things up sartorially, changing into a head-to-toe grey ensemble from Valentino’s pre-fall 2023 collection; a pairing of an oversized jumper and a feathered floor-length skirt.

Raymond Hall//Getty Images

Feathers also featured in her next outfit change. The actress – who works alongside her stylist Leith Clark – put a fashionable spin on the capsule classic trench coat, with a feather-trimmed Valentino camel number worn with patent Mary-Jane flats.

Raymond Hall//Getty Images

Come evening, she stepped out in a sheer black Chanel jumpsuit with shimmering polka dots, worn with matching black platforms that gave it a Seventies feel. Saving the biggest show-stopper for last, Knightley finished her well-dressed 24 hours with a golden Simone Rocha midi-dress with playful puffed sleeves.

Getty Images

NBC//Getty Images

The actress is promoting her latest film Boston Strangler, in which she plays the real-life investigative reporter Loretta McLaughlin – who worked on the infamous case in the 1960s, where 13 women were tragically and brutally murdered.

‘Boston Strangler’ is released on Disney+ on 17 March
