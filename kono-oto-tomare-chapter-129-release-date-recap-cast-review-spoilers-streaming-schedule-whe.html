Exploring Kono Oto Tomare!’s Story

Let’s dive into the world of Kono Oto Tomare! This manga, lovingly penned by Sakura Amyuu, unfolds the journey of the Koto Music Club at Takezou Kurata’s high school. Facing the risk of disbandment, Takezou’s fate takes a twist when the troublemaker Chika Kudou invites him to join. Alongside skilled koto player Satowa Houzuki and other passionate members, they aim to become the nation’s best.
