Sia Leaks Her Nude Photo Ahead Of Paparazzi

The "Chandelier" singer has upped her game when it comes to fighting against the paparazzi. In a recent attempt for the stalker photographers to sell her nude photos to the press for a stack of cash, Sia has beaten them to it by posting the pics to the web, free of charge. 

Taking to Twitter, the songstress shares a picture of herself nude, with views from behind adding the caption, "Someone is apparently trying to sell naked photos of me to my fans. Save your money, here it is for free. Everyday is Christmas!"

The Twitterverse went into a bit of frenzy praising the singer for sharing her figure, with one user saying, "When @Sia is life." 

Some were quick to point out the fact that the "Elastic Heart" singer, who is famous for covering up her face, was quick to show her backside before she ever revealed her true identity to fans.
