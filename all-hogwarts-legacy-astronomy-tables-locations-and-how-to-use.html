How To Find All Astronomy Tables In Hogwarts Legacy

Although Hogwarts Legacy is not the largest open-world game in the market, it is big enough for players to get lost in. This is especially true if you are looking for a certain item in the game.  Here are the locations for the Astronomy Tables in Hogwarts Legacy:

  • Southern Hogwarts near the castle turret
  • Southern Hogwarts opposite side of the castle
  • Hogwarts Castle
  • Hogwarts Valley near ruins by a river
  • Hogwarts Valley near the woods
  • North Ford Bog
  • Forbidden Forest
  • Hogsmeade Valley
  • Feldcroft Region on a clifftop near balloons
  • Feldcroft Region western edge of the map
  • Clagmar Coast
  • Cragcroftshire
  • Poldsear Coast
  • Marunweem Lake
  • Manor Cape

Thankfully, there is a map to help players understand where the Astronomy Tables are in Hogwarts Legacy. To find Astronomy Tables, players can simply look at their map as they will be marked there. Go to the locations as listed above, pull out your map, and you should be able to pinpoint each location of the Astronomy Tables.

How To Use Astronomy Tables In Hogwarts Legacy

To use the Astronomy Tables, players will first need to complete the Astronomy Class to learn how to use them in the first place.

It's also worth noting that Astronomy Tables are only useable when the moon is out in Hogwarts Legacy. So it might be worth knowing how to change the time of day.

Clear both of these checks, and you are good to go hunt for Astronomy Tables. Once you find one, a constellation of stars should appear which you will need to align to solve the puzzle.

Hogwarts Legacy Astronomy Tables Rewards

If you are aiming for 100% Hogwarts Legacy, then finding all the Astronomy Tables in Hogwarts Legacy is on the to-do list. It is well worth your time to find all the Astronomy Tables in the game as you will be rewarded with a couple of cosmetic items. Players will get the Starry-Eyed Seer's Scarf, the Starry-Eyed Seer's Ensemble, and the Starry-Eyed Seer's Cape.

All Hogwarts Legacy Astronomy Tables Locations and How To Use FAQ

Where can I find all the Astronomy Tables in Hogwarts Legacy?

Astronomy Tables can be found in various locations across Hogwarts Legacy, including Southern Hogwarts, Hogwarts Castle, Hogwarts Valley, Forbidden Forest, Hogsmeade Valley, and more. Consult your in-game map to easily locate them.

How do I use Astronomy Tables in Hogwarts Legacy?

Complete the Astronomy Class to learn how to use Astronomy Tables. They can only be used when the moon is out. Align the constellation of stars that appears at the table to solve the puzzle.

What rewards can I get from finding all the Astronomy Tables?

Finding all the Astronomy Tables in Hogwarts Legacy rewards players with cosmetic items such as the Starry-Eyed Seer's Scarf, Starry-Eyed Seer's Ensemble, and Starry-Eyed Seer's Cape. This is especially worthwhile for players aiming for 100% completion.
