12 Creative Photoshoot For Boyfriend Ideas

4. A photo worth a thousand words

It’s one thing to tell your boyfriend you love him. But how about showing him a photo worth a thousand words? Include meaningful details in your photo like wearing his jacket, taking pictures in his favorite place, or posing with his favorite things.

For instance, if your boyfriend is a gearhead, we bet he would love to have photos of you posing with his dream car. Some Peerspace venues, like this one in Miami, even come with luxury cars available to use when you book! And not just any luxury cars. We’re talkin’ Rolls Royce, Lambos, Bentleys, and Ferrari.

5. Highlighting the qualities he loves about you most

Is It your creative spirit? Your sense of adventure? What is it about you that drives your boyfriend wild? Whatever that special quality is — capitalize on it in your photoshoot!

For example, if your boyfriend loves how wild, spontaneous, and creative you are, then he’s going to absolutely love the photos you take in a space like this Los Angeles art installation and gallery. It’s a magical 1,249-square-foot space brimming with psychedelic colors and art. Your photos will look amazing, especially thanks to the abundant natural light, high ceilings, and open layout of the studio.

6. Memories of where you met

Where did you and your main squeeze meet? Wherever that may be, having your photo sesh in this location makes for fantastic photoshoot for boyfriend ideas! If the specific spot where you first met up isn’t ideal for a photoshoot, broaden the idea and take photos in the city or town where you met that best represents your relationship.

Raleigh-based couples may want to rent a venue like this downtown condo that offers panoramic views of the city skyline where you went on your first date! If you book enough time, you can even stick around after the photographer leaves and cook dinner together and enjoy it over candlelight on the condo’s balcony.

7. All this photo is missing is you

Speaking of turning photoshoot into a date night, why not delight your boyfriend doing just that, but with a solo shoot?! Pick a Peerspace photoshoot venue that can double as a romantic date night spot. Then, take some sexy, fun, and creative photos there to share with your main squeeze.

We love using this mysterious speakeasy in Austin as inspiration. When you share the photos with him, include a note that says, “all this photo is missing is you. Date night?” We bet he’ll love seeing the venue you took your sexy shots in person!

8. Swing away

Don’t just take any old pictures for your boyfriend — get truly creative with the pics you snap! For example, what about booking a venue like this Atlanta content creation studio that comes with an LED cloud room, an adorable giant swing, a graffiti wall, and more. You’ll have so many opportunities for beautiful pics that you’ll never want to leave!

“Honestly, Speechless,” shares a Peerspace reviewer. “I was so impressed with the set I chose and it is what it say it is. Once I was finish shooting I had the opportunity to look a other sets two of which I know I will be using in the future!”

9. A sultry milk bath shoot

Combine the sexiness of a boudoir shoot with the originality of totally unique photoshoot for your boyfriend idea and what do you get? A sultry milk bath shoot! This rustic, charming greenhouse with natural light in Leander, Texas is perfect for just such a shoot. It’s a private outdoor space that lets you enjoy your milk bath photoshoot en plein air.

But remember, there are many other options for milk bath shoots around the U.S. and beyond as well! Check out all the options on Peerspace. 

Go full-on sultry by diving into these 11 burlesque photoshoot ideas!
