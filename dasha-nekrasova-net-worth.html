Co-host of the popular podcast Red Scare which features interviews and discusses politics, feminism and socialism. She is popular on Instagram where she has over 100,000 followers.

She attended Las Vegas Academy of Arts in high school and then attended Mills College and studied sociology and philosophy. She was first made a public figure in 2018 when she was interviewed on the program Infowars where she was dubbed "Sailor Socialism" in a clip that went viral. She started the podcast Red Scare with co-host Anna Khachiyan in March 2018.

She is an actress and director. She appeared in a music video by the alternative band Yumi Zouma. She made her film debut as the co-writer and actress in the film Wobble Palace. She has had a recurring role as Comfry in season three of the HBO show Succession.
