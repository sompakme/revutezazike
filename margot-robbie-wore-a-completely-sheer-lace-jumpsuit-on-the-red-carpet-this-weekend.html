Margot Robbie wore a completely sheer lace jumpsuit on the red carpet this weekend

Margot Robbie turned heads in a completely sheer Chanel look on the red carpet this weekend. The actress – who was attending the premiere of Dreamland during the Tribeca Film Festival – wore a wide-leg, black, lace jumpsuit and paired it with a fluffy cardigan.

Usually one to keep it fairly demure on the red carpet, the look was a step outside of Robbie's style comfort zone, but she pulled it off expertly.

The actress added a chunky sparkly belt to the look and teamed the jumpsuit with black heels and a mohair knit.

Dreamland is Robbie's latest film from Lucky Chap Entertainment, the production company she runs alongside husband Tom Ackerley. The film is a period action drama thriller set in Texas during the depression, which stars Robbie alongside Finn Cole, Travis Fimmel, Kerry Condon, Garrett Hedlund and Darby Camp.

Robbie recently returned from a holiday with Ackerley in the Caribbean, a glimpse of which the notoriously private couple shared on her Instagram with a photos of some polaroids from their trip.
