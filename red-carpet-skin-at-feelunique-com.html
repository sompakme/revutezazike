Red carpet skin at FeelUnique.com

We are loving the glamour of awards season but if you, like us, have been wondering how the A-list stay looking so fresh-faced despite their globe-trotting schedules, we have the answer.

Kinara Spa has been pampering Tinseltowns finest faces for the last 10 years in its exclusive West Hollywood location, but now thanks to the folks at FeelUnique.com, you can enjoy the luxury skincare brand at home for the first time in the UK.

Kinara means on the edge in Hindi, and the company prides itself on living up to its name, with the likes of Anne Hathaway, Natalie Portman, Halle Berry and Jennifer Aniston all enjoying the fusion of luxury and cutting-edge treatments which leave them with photo-perfect skin.

To prepare yourself for your close-up, stock up on the capsule line of beauty must-haves, which are packed with active, skin-respecting ingredients. Bazaar loves the velvety-rich Kinara Reparative Nurient Serum, which is packed with brightening, antioxidant Marula Oil, soothing Rose Hip Seed Oil and a collagen-boosting (ie. fine line-firming) Peptide complex. And thanks to Kinara founder Olga Lorencin-Northrups dedication to meeting the multi-faceted demands of every possible skin type, this powerful serum is still gentle enough for dry and sensitive skin.  

Even better, in homage to its celeb following, Kinara will soon launch its Red Carpet Facial kit in the UK, also at FeelUnique.com. Consisting of products used in their signature treatments, expect award-winning instant radiance at the click of a button from the end of the February  just in time for the Oscars.
