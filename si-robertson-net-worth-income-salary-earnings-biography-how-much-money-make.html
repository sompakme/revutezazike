Silas Merritt Robertson, better known by his stage as Si Robertson, is an American actor, hunter, preacher, and television personality. Si Robertson is most known to the general public for his role in the reality television series “Duck Dynasty,” which debuted on television screens in 201The programme, which was co-produced by Deirdre Gurney and Scott Gurney, is about the Robertson family and their effort to run a duck and buck call manufacturing company called “Duck Commander.” The first season of “Duck Dynasty” averaged 1.82 million viewers per episode, while the sixth season averaged 3.77 million people per episode.  The series’ Christmas special episode became the most viewed episode in the history of the “A&R” television network, while the season four opener drew 11.8 million viewers, the most ever for a single episode of the programme. The Robertson family has been on a variety of other television programmes as a consequence of the success of “Duck Dynasty,” including “The Wendy Williams Show,” “The Tonight Show with Jay Leno,” and “Live! with Kelly and Michael,” to name a few. “Duck Dynasty” also prompted the production of “Duck the Halls: A Robertson Family Christmas,” which debuted at #1 on the Top Country Albums list and has since sold over 750,000 copies.
