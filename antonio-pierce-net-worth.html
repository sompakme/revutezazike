Antonio Pierce Net Worth

Antonio Pierce was a Super Bowl-winning linebacker who was born on October 26, 1978 in Ontario, CA. He spent most of his career with the New York Giants, where he recorded 686 tackles over nine years. Pierce was a key part of the Giants' Super Bowl XLII victory in 2008, and was named to the Pro Bowl in 2006.
