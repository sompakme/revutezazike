Nicole Scherzinger rumoured to be dating Serena Williams' ex Grigor Dimitrov

    Nicole Scherzinger may have found her perfect match following her split from Lewis Hamilton. The 37-year-old has reportedly been dating tennis ace Grigor Dimitrov since he split from Maria Sharapova in July.

    "Nicole is dating Grigor. They're completely besotted with each other," a source told . "But they've been careful not to be pictured together because they don't want the pressure of the relationship being in the public eye."

    Nicole Scherzinger is reportedly dating Grigor Dimitrov

    The source also said that Formula One Champion Lewis isn't happy that Nicole has moved on.

    "Lewis knows and is very unhappy," they told the newspaper. "It's made him realise he isn't over her. He's been desperately trying to talk to Nicole over the last few weeks."

    Nicole and Lewis recently sparked rumours of a reconciliation after they were both spotted partying at The Box in Soho on a night out, however a rep for the former Pussycat Doll said they were not back together and it was a "coincidence" that they had been out in the same place.

    Grigor previously dated Maria Sharapova and Serena Williams

    Indeed the singer was still insisting that she was single during an appearance on on Thursday, telling host Philip Schofield that dating is "not where my focus is, I've just been so busy with everything".

    While Nicole and Lewis ended their seven-year relationship in February, Grigor has been single after calling time on his two-year relationship with fellow Tennis player Maria Sharapova in July. The 24-year-old, who also dated Serena Williams in 2012, said that he was ready to focus on his tennis career following his split from Maria.

    "Our paths split," he said. "We experienced wonderful moments together. I wish her much happiness and success in life and tennis. Now I'm concentrated entirely on the game and I'm sure the results will be seen. This is the summer of new beginning for me."
