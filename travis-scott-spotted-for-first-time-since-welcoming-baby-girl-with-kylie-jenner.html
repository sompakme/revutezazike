Travis Scott Spotted For First Time Since Welcoming Baby Girl With Kylie Jenner

Just days after the rumours were finally put to rest and Travis Scott and Kylie Jenner welcomed their baby girl into the world, the "Antidote" rapper has been spotted leaving Kylie's Calabasas mansion.

Although he doesn't seem too pleased to be having his photo snapped, Travis was all smiles in the video Kylie shared of her pregnancy where a clip shows Travis holding his baby girl in awe. Page Six  reports that Travis still managed to make an appearance at a planned gig this past Saturday at Marquee in Las Vegas.

Sources tell the publication that Travis performed a 25-minute set and stayed for a short amount of time afterwards chilling with Jim Jones and Cam’ron. “He was in a good mood,” the source reveals. “Everything went perfectly on time. He gave no clues to the audience about the birth.”

Kylie's older sister Kim, shared a touching message for her sister and how she will be the "the best mom." With Kim having just welcomed her new baby girl only two weeks ago, she says she "can’t wait for them to be besties."

"I am so proud that you do what you want to do and live your life the way you want," she added. "You will pass down all of those amazing qualities to your baby girl. You were made to be a mom! I love you!"
