Incantation Scarab - Elden Ring Guide

The Incantation Scarab is an Armor piece available in Elden Ring, and is not part of any known armor set.

Description

"Golden scarab worn directly on the head. These scarab roll clumps of incantations during their labors. Slightly reduces FP cost of incantations, but increases damage taken. As a scarab approaches death, it abandons its rolled treasure and stretches its wings wide for the long journey to its home nest."

Locations Found / Sold

  • This armor can be found as a reward for solving the Homing Instinct Painting puzzle located in East Limgrave's Artist Shack, depicing a painting you'll need to find the source of - located among the ruins north of the Seaside Ruins Site of Grace in West Limgrave.
