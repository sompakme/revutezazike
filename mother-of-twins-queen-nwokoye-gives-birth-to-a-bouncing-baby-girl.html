Mother of twins, Queen Nwokoye, gives birth to a bouncing baby girl

- Nollywood actress, Queen Nwokoye, welcomes third child with husband

- The actress' friend, Oma Nanadi, congratulated her on social media

- Queen tied the knot with her husband, Oluchi Uzoma, in 2011

Congratulations are in order for Nollywood actress, Queen Nwokoye and her husband, Oluchi Uzoma, as they welcome their third child. The couple tied the knot in 2011 and were proud parents of twins before they welcomed a new addition to their family.

Nwokoye's friend, Oma Nnadi, shared the news on social media while congratulating her for becoming a new mother. She also shared old photos of the actress when she was still pregnant with her baby girl whom she delivered recently.

Queen is yet to announce she's a new mother to her fans on social media but many people have been pouring in their congratulatory messages since Oma made it known that the actress delivered her new bundle of joy safely.

Nwokoye was born in Lagos state into a catholic family but she hails from Ihembosi in Ekwusigo local government of Anambra state Nigeria.

The 36-year-old actress started her education at Air Force Primary School. She completed her secondary school education at Queen's College, Enugu, before she proceeded to Nnamdi Azikiwe University, Awka, where she studied sociology and anthropology.

Although queen grew up with the ambition of becoming a lawyer, she delved into the entertainment industry and has been enjoying it ever since.
