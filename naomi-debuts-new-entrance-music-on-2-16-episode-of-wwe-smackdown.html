Naomi Debuts New Entrance Music on 2/16 Episode of WWE SmackDown

Naomi went all around the world to find herself back and right after doing so, she came back to the place she has always considered her home, WWE at the 2024 Royal Rumble event.

This week on SmackDown, The GLOW returned to in-ring action on the blue brand after a long time as part of her second coming in the WWE. However, she chose to start this new resurgence in a completely new fashion.

As witnessed, the former SmackDown Women’s champion debuted a new entrance music. It began with her name initials appearing in bold letters one by one on the titantron followed by her trademark neon glow lights illuminating the entire arena.

Naomi entered the Delta Center in Salt Lake City with a thunderous response as she made her way to the ring to face young upstart, Alba Fyre. She replaced Naomi’s original opponent, Shotzi who was sidelined with an injury this past week on WWE NXT’s tapings for the following edition.

Both superstars battled each other on tonight’s edition of SmackDown to earn a coveted spot in the Women’s Elimination Chamber match taking place at the name-sake event in Perth, Australia next week.
