Is a refrigerator colder at 1 o’clock or 9 o’clock?

Inside the fresh food section of certain Bottom Freezer refrigerators are manual knob temperature controls. “9” is the coldest setting, while “1” is the hottest. Warmer temperatures are achieved by turning knobs to lower numbers, whereas colder temperatures are achieved by turning knobs to higher ones.

What is the procedure for resetting the defrost button on a refrigerator?
