Jailer On OTT: Release Date Announced Officially

Superstar Rajinikanth’s latest blockbuster, “Jailer,” directed by Nelson Dilipkumar, has taken the box office by storm. As the movie’s theatrical run nears its end, the eagerly awaited OTT release date has been revealed. “Jailer” is set to premiere on Amazon Prime Video on September 7.

“Jailer” features an ensemble cast, including Tamannaah Bhatia, Ramya Krishna, Jackie Shroff, Mirnaa Menon, Sunil, Naga Babu, Yogi Babu, and Vasanth Ravi, with crucial cameos by Mohanlal and Shiv Rajkumar. The film is produced by Sun Pictures and boasts a musical score by Anirudh Ravichander.

The film will be available in multiple languages, including Tamil, Telugu, Hindi, Malayalam, and Kannada.

With just one more week to go, the fans want to experience Jailer one last time in theatres after which, they can watch the film at the comfort of their homes.
