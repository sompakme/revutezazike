What are the 12 months in French? The names of the months in French are "janvier" 'January', "février" 'February', "mars" 'March', "avril" 'April', "mai" 'May', "juin" 'June', "juillet" 'July', "août" 'August', "septembre" 'September', "octobre" 'October', "novembre" 'November' and "décembre" 'December'.
